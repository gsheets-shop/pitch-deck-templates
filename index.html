Our Manifesto

Not all templates are made equally.

There are thousands of pitch deck templates to choose from online, but which ones can you actually trust?

  • Some look visually-stunning, but are missing substance.
  • Others are tedious to edit (ie. the "graphs" are actually shapes).
  • Most templates don't even cover all the critical slides required for a complete pitch!

As founders who have personally been through the process of raising from VCs, we know time is the last thing you have to spare, so we hope this resource saves you some of the legwork. Having created hundreds of pitch decks that collectively closed billions, we have first-hand experience with what it takes to succeed (and how to spot a template that sets you up for failure).

This is our hand-vetted collection of the best pitch deck templates broken down by industry, funding, stage, and software. We'll keep this updated regularly with new finds!
